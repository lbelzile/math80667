1 Principles of the design of experiment

  • Why perform experiments? Science is driven by empirical findings and these can provide insights on theories.

However, science is plagued with bad science. Reproducibility crisis

The objective of this course is to equip with you with simple techniques for answering simple questions. However, many key concepts can be useful. This course emphasizes open science as a plateform for research. We also highligh throughout notes potential pitfalls and statistical fallacies so you don’t fall in the traps.
